SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS



Goal 7: Affordable and Clean Energy

  • General Description

    • Goal 7 of the SDGs, "Affordable and Clean Energy," aims to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. This goal recognizes that energy is essential for economic development, human well-being, and environmental sustainability. It emphasizes the need to transition to cleaner and more efficient energy sources to address climate change and promote sustainable development.

  • Targets

    • Universal Access : Ensure universal access to affordable, reliable, and modern energy services by expanding infrastructure and increasing the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix.

    • Renewable Energy: Increase substantially the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix, with a focus on enhancing energy efficiency and increasing the use of renewable sources.

    • Energy Efficiency: Double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency in various sectors, including transportation, industry, and households.

    • Sustainable Infrastructure: Enhance international cooperation to facilitate access to clean energy research, technology, and investment, and promote the development of energy infrastructure and clean technology.

  • Progress


    • Significant progress has been made in expanding access to electricity in various regions, particularly in urban areas. The use of renewable energy sources like solar and wind has also increased, contributing to a more diverse energy mix and reduced greenhouse gas emissions.However, challenges remain in ensuring universal access to clean energy, particularly in rural and remote areas. The transition to renewable energy sources requires continued investment in technology, infrastructure, and policy frameworks. In addition, energy efficiency efforts need to be accelerated to reduce energy consumption and mitigate environmental impacts.

    • Achieving Goal 7 involves collaboration between governments, private sector, and civil society to promote sustainable energy systems. It requires balancing energy access with environmental sustainability, while also considering affordability and social equity.
